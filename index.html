Welcome to the UofT Tennis Club!

We are an active and friendly organization that takes pride in bringing all of the fun and action of our beloved sport to the University of Toronto community. Whether you play competitively, for fun or just want to try it out, we as a club offer a chance for you to enjoy the game as much as we do.

Join our Facebook page to stay up to date and to find other members to hit with. We look forward to seeing you out on the courts!

UTSU Clubs Fair and Street Fest 2016 - 2017

August 23, 2016

The UofT Tennis Club is bringing you a ton of action on and off the court this year! We welcome players of all levels, including those who have never picked up a racket. Here's a preview of what we'll be offering this year:

✓ Intramural League

✓ Ladder Play
