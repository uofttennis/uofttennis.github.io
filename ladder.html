Welcome to the University of Toronto St. George Tennis ladder!

The U of T tennis ladder is open to players of any skill. We promote a fun and competitive setting to help active members improve their skills and meet other players. The ladder will be active for a period of 3 months, before it resets. The ladder will be updated on a weekly basis. Ladder Rules and Regulations

This ranking system of this ladder is the same as the Leap Frog system. When a lower ranked player beats a higher ranked player, the lower ranked player "leap frogs" over the higher ranked player. If the higher ranked player wins, nothing happens. Rules and regulations:

1. This session will be from Jan____ to Mar_____

2. Only payed members of the U of T tennis club may join the ladder

3. Challenges will be issued by individuals through Facebook

4. Players may only challenge individuals 5 ranks above them

5. Players issuing a challenge must give at least 2 different times to play the match

6. The match must be played within a week of the challenge

7. Declining a challenge will be counted as a forfeit, unless a sufficient reason is given

8. Players may only have one pending challenge

9. Players have 48 hours to accept the pending challenge

10. Matches will be played in best of 2 sets:

---->Each set is first to win 6 or more games with a margin of 2 games. A tie-breaker is played if each team has won 6 games.

---->Example scores: 6-3, 5-7, 6-4 or 6-4, 7-6

11. The challenger is responsible to bring a set of balls

12. Players who have been inactive on the ladder for over 3 weeks will be removed from the ladder for the current session
