Welcome to the University of Toronto St. George Tennis ladder!

The U of T tennis ladder is open to players of any skill. We promote a fun and competitive setting to help active members improve their skills and meet other players. The ladder will be active for a period of 3 months, before it resets. The ladder will be updated on a weekly basis. By signing up for the ladder, please understand that we expect you to participate in playing matches. Please play at least one match every two weeks. Ladder position
